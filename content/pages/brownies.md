title: Brownies

Stir dry ingredients together
2 cups flour 
2 cups sugar
½ cup cocoa
1 ½ tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt 

Add:
3 eggs, 2 tsp vanilla,
1 cup oil, ½ cup milk
Mix well and stir in1 ½  cup chopped nuts (optional).

Pour into a well greased and flour 9x13 pan and Bake at 350° for 30 min.  
Cool and frost if desired.
