title: Buttermilk Chocolate Cake
tags: desserts

LaRue Phillips

Melt together:

* 1 stick margarine
* 1 cup water
* 4 Tbs cocoa (may reduce)

Mix together:

* 2 cups flour
* 2 cups sugar

Mix the two mixtures above then add:

* 1/2 cup buttermilk
* 2 eggs slightly beaten
* 1 tsp soda
* 1 tsp vanilla

Bake in  9"x13" pan at 400Â° for 30 min.
