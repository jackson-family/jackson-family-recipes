title: Baked Chicken & Rice Casserole

LaRue Phillips 

* 1-1/2 cups rice (uncooked)
* 4 to 6 chicken breasts (or cut up chicken pieces)
* 1 10-oz can Cream of Mushroom soup
* 2 10-oz cans water
* 1 pkg dry onion soup mix
			
Put the raw rice in the bottom of a large baking dish.  Pour in soup and water.  Mix well.  Put in the chicken breasts.  Sprinkle dry onion soup over breasts.  Cover with foil and bake 1-1/2 hours at 325Â°.
