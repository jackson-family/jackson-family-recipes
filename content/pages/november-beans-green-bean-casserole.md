title: November Beans (Green Bean Casserole)
tags: salads/vegetables/appetizers

* 3/4 cup milk
* 1/8 tsp pepper
* 1 10-oz can cream of mushroom soup
* 1 tsp Worchestershire sauce
* 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
* 2 14-oz cans cut green beans (drained)
* 1-1/3 cups French's french fried onions

In 1-1/2 qt casserole mix first 5 ingredients and 2/3 cup of french fried onions.  Add green beans and stir in gently.  Bake 30 min at 350Â° or until hot.  Stir.  Top with 2/3 cup onions.  Bake 5 min or until onions are golden brown.
