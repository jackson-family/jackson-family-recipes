title: Banana Nut Bread
tags: breads

LaRue Phillips

Ingredients

* 1/2 cup shortening
* 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
* 1/2 cup white sugar
* 2 eggs
* 1 cup mashed bananas (approx 2 large)
* 1-1/2 cups flour
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 tsp soda
* 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions

Cream shortening and sugars.  Add eggs and beat well.  Mash and add bananas.  Sift together dry ingredients and fold into batter.

Roll the nuts in flour and stir into batter.  Bake 350Â° for 30-45 min or until a tooth pick inserted into the center comes out clean.
