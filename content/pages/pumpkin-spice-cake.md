title: Pumpkin Spice Cake
tags: desserts

Ellen Jackson

* 2 cups flour 
* 2 tsp baking powder
* 1 tsp baking soda
* 1 tsp salt
* 2 tsp cinnamon
* 1-3/4 cup granulated sugar

Add:

* 4 eggs
* 2 cups pumpkin
* 1 cup oil

Blend in 1/2 cup chopped nuts

Grease and flour bunt pan.  Pour in mixture and bake for about 50 min in 350Â° preheated oven.  Toothpick inserted into cake should come out clean when cake is done.  Very good plain, sprinkled with powered sugar, or frosted with a cream cheese frosting. 

Frosting:

* 4 oz cream cheese
* 1/2 cup margarine
* 4 cups powdered sugar
* 1 to 2 Tbs milk
* 1/2 tsp vanilla

Mix with mixer.  Frost when cake is cool.
